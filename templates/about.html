About the Project

Machine learning should
be accessible.

AutoML Platform was built on one belief — the gap between a raw dataset and a production-ready model should be measured in minutes, not weeks of engineering.

Mission

Why we built this.

Most ML tools force a choice: use a black-box AutoML service and lose all visibility, or write everything from scratch and spend a week on boilerplate before touching your data.

AutoML Platform sits between those extremes. It's fully open and transparent — every model, metric, and hyperparameter is visible — but it handles all repetitive work automatically. Preprocessing, cross-validation, hyperparameter search, visualization, and PDF reports are all built in.

We also believe deployment is part of the ML workflow, not an afterthought. Every trained model comes with a live REST API, drift detection, and ONNX export for non-Python runtimes — all out of the box.
